Real Housewives of Lagos: An Analysis of Twitter's Perception

In this project, I performed an analysis of over 170,000 tweets on Real Housewives of Lagos reality TV show using Natural Language Processing techniques in Python and developed a dashboard using Power BI.

Analysis of International Debt Statistics

In this project, I analyzed a dataset provided by The World Bank using SQL, which includes information about the debt (in USD) owed by various developing countries across multiple categories. The aim of this analysis was to uncover insights regarding international debt, helping us understand how much debt different countries owe and in what categories.

Maven Toys Store Expansion: Data-Driven Insights for Strategic Growth

In this project, I developed a business intelligence dashboard for Maven Toys to support their expansion strategy with data-driven insights. The dashboard is designed to offer a comprehensive view of key business metrics, allowing stakeholders to make informed decisions about where and how to grow the business.

Supplier Quality & Performance Dashboard

In this project, I analyzed supplier quality data for a manufacturing company to identify areas for improvement. The dashboard I created visualizes defect quantities and downtime across different vendors, plants, and material types. This analysis helps the company make data-driven decisions to enhance supplier performance and reduce operational inefficiencies.

Insurance Complaints Report

This project involved analyzing complaint procedure data for a fictional insurance company. The analysis focused on complaint trends over the past two years, identifying problematic brokers, and evaluating customer satisfaction. Through this project, I utilized multiple data sources to provide a comprehensive audit of the company's complaint handling process.

Marketing Analytics

For this project, I delved into a dataset containing marketing metrics. My goal was to uncover key trends and patterns that could inform future marketing strategies. The analysis covered customer segmentation, campaign performance, and ROI, providing actionable insights to optimize marketing efforts.
